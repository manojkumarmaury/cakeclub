About Us

Bill Tech is a name that stands for quality, convenience and efficiency in the domain of electrical products. It has always believed in serving its customers with commitment and passion through its futuristic outlook, responsible technology & reliable products that not just lead to energy conservation, but to a sustainable world ahead.

Throughout the years, Bill Tech team has lived up to its long drawn culture of dedication and constant innovation. Its products exemplify the convergence of safety and style to suit every home, office, showroom or any commercial set up. The range includes a variety of electrical products ie.; LED lightings, low voltage switchgears, modular wiring accessories and more, which enjoy great demand within India and abroad.
